About the role

Job Description
  1. Pre-anesthetic assessment and preparation of the patients.
  2. Provision of clinical anesthesia services in operation theatre both routine and emergency
  3. Supervision of management of post-anesthetic care, including surgical ICU care delivered by juniors such as monitoring including clinical observations, pain relief, fluid therapy, respiratory therapy, adverse effects with may be related to Anesthesia, preoperative medical problems
  4. Supervision of paramedical staffs in OT, Cath Lab & ICU.
